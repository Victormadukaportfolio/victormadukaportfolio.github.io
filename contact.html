Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start on my project?

I typically begin new projects within 2-3 business days after the initial consultation and agreement. For urgent projects, same-day starts are possible.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. For example, a simple website takes 1-2 weeks, while larger projects may take 4-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes! I provide 24/7 support and maintenance packages to ensure your digital solutions continue to perform optimally long after launch.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept bank transfers, mobile payments, and international payments via PayPal, Grey, Payoneer or CashApp. Payment is typically structured as 50% upfront and 50% upon completion.

Do you work with clients outside Nigeria?

Absolutely! While I'm based in Lagos Nigeria, I work with clients worldwide. All communication can be handled remotely via email, Whatsapp, video calls, and project management tools.

Can you help with an existing website or project?

Yes, I can take over, improve, or maintain existing projects. I'll assess the current state and provide recommendations for optimization and growth.
